The classic edition of Fall Dash, first released in 2016 as an experimental game.

Fall through outer space, avoid obstacles, gain speed and points. Fly down as far as possible.

How to play

Gain FTL speed. The higher your speed, the more points you get. Get as many as you can! Beware of obstacles, don't fly off the screen or you will lose.
